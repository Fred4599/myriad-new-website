## Key Themes & Positioning

The client wants to emphasize:

- **Community Over Service** — Positioning as a collaborative community rather than just a marketing vendor
- **Cost Sharing** — Highlighting the ability to share costs with other local businesses
- **Work-Life Balance** — Strong focus on helping entrepreneurs "recapture their life" while growing revenue
- **Trust & Relationships** — Emphasizing the personal, no-pressure approach with 1:1 meetings
- **Exclusivity** — Option to ensure they don't work with competitors
- **Flexibility** — No contracts, cancel anytime, results guaranteed
- **Comprehensive Services** — From basic marketing to advanced automation and AI integration
